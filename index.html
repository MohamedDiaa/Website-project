Antimicrobial coatings

The Corona pandemic with increasing confirmed cases challenges us. New hygiene concepts are needed. Before Covid-19 in mid-March, simple cleaning and disinfection was sufficient. But shortly after wiping, new colonization can occur. 99% of the time, a surface is exposed to viruses and germs until the next disinfection occurs. What if there was a surface that could defend itself against certain viruses around the clock? Our antimicrobial coating inactivates the Sars cov-2 coronavirus 10x faster, providing additional protection against the communicable disease.

Ceramic sealants

Ceramic sealants protect against UV radiation on a molecular basis. A three-dimensional crystal lattice allows water to roll off due to lower surface tension. The protection remains on the entire paint if you use a ph-neutral cleaner. Normally, the end user has to have his car sealed. We additionally offer consumer products of ceramic coatings.

Nano coating

“Nano” (Greek: dwarf) is the part of a billionth of a meter. Ultra-thin coatings prevent limescale from forming on shower stalls in the bathroom, for example, as with nano glass sealing, or simply allow wine to roll off upholstery and T-shirts. Nano sealings have no effect on UV radiation – wood undergoes its characteristic aging as desired, but remains free of mold and moss.

Learn more

Waterproofing spray

Clothing or shoes waterproof have always been at the expense of nature in the past. Per- and polyfluorinated chemicals in waterproofing spray polluted the environment. However, we have an ecologically sustainable solution that works in wind and weather. Sprayed on at a distance of 30 centimeters, our impregnation spray technology works reliably after drying and is suitable for all textiles and outdoor shoes. Gore Tex and Sympatex membranes can be re-impregnated once the effect wears off.
